About me

I am currently an incoming Ph.D. student at AI Thrust, Information Hub, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), where I am supervised by Prof. Hao Liu. Before that, I received the M.Eng. degree in Computer Technology from Central South University in 2022, advised by Prof. Jianliang Gao. I obtained a B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Engineering, from Bohai University. My research focuses on Graph Representation Learning, specifically the area of Graph Neural Networks including their theoretical foundations and applications.
