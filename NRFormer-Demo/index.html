Radiation Forecasting Heatmap

  • Nuclear radiation Heatmap
     The image on the left displays a heat map illustrating the daily radiation changes in Japan as predicted by our NRFormer.

  • Features
     1. Automatically visualize daily radiation forecast changes for Japan.
    2. Hover over the mouse to reveal the names of the prefectures.
    3. The current date of predicted data is shown in the upper left corner of the map.
    4. Effectively illustrates the dispersion of radiation.
