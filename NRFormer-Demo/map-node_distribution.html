How to use this system

  • Data Characteristics:
    1. Scroll up or down to zoom in or out of the map.
    2. Drag the map to center and maximize the display of the relevant prefecture.
    3. Detailed spatial distribution of radiation monitoring stations.

  • Predictive Visualization
    1. Click Select Date to start forecasting. (can only select times between July 5, 2023, and December 18, 2023.)
    2. Select the radiation monitoring station you wish to view for prediction.
    2. Drag the prediction box to the desired position for viewing.
    3. Click on the empty space to dismiss the display of the prediction box.

  • Notes:
    1. All forecast data are for 2023. The year is omitted for convenience.
